## SPENDING ACCOUNTS — Administered by Igoe (800.633.8818 opt 1 | goigoe.com | flex@goigoe.com)

### Health FSA (Medical Care Reimbursement Account)
- Covers out-of-pocket medical, dental, vision, Rx expenses for employee and tax dependents
- **2025/2026 Max Election:** $3,300 | Carryover: up to $660
- **2026/2027 Max Election:** $3,400 | Carryover: up to $680
- Plan Year: 8/1–7/31 | Run Out Period ends 10/31
- Can NOT be combined with HSA (use LPFSA instead if enrolled in HDHP)

### Limited Purpose FSA (LPFSA) — For HDHP/HSA enrollees
- Covers dental, vision, and orthodontia expenses only (and medical after HDHP deductible met)
- **2025/2026 Max:** $3,300 | Carryover: $660
- **2026/2027 Max:** $3,400 | Carryover: $680

### Health Savings Account (HSA) — HDHP enrollees only
- **2025 IRS Contribution Limits:** $4,300 individual / $8,550 family
- Administered by Igoe: goigoe.com | 800.633.8818 option 1
- Funds roll over year to year; portable if you leave
- Triple tax advantage: pre-tax contributions, tax-free growth, tax-free withdrawals for qualified expenses
- Invest funds once balance exceeds $1,000
- Eligible expenses: copays, deductibles, prescriptions, dental, vision, medical equipment, OTC medications

### Dependent Care FSA
- Covers day care, after-school care, elder care for tax dependents
- **2025/2026 Max:** $5,000 | Grace Period through 10/15/2026
- **2026/2027 Max:** $7,500 | Grace Period through 10/15/2027

### Commuter Benefits
- Pre-tax dollars for mass transit (buses, subways, trains, tokens/vouchers) and parking near work or transit
- Monthly maximums set by IRS — check goigoe.com for current limits
- Use Benefits Card or submit for reimbursement

## IMPORTANT NOTES FOR AIMEE

1. **Plan Year Transition:** The 2025/2026 plan year ends July 31, 2026. The 2026/2027 plan year starts August 1, 2026. Open Enrollment runs June 17–30, 2026.
2. **Carrier Change:** Medical switched from UnitedHealthcare (2025/2026) to Anthem Blue Cross (2026/2027) for EPO/HDHP/PPO. Kaiser HMO continues unchanged.
3. **Premiums increased** from 2025/2026 to 2026/2027 across all medical tiers.
4. **Dental and voluntary benefit rates are the same** in both plan years.
5. **HDHP paired benefits:** HDHP enrollees can open an HSA (tax-advantaged savings) but CANNOT have a standard Health FSA — use the Limited Purpose FSA instead.
6. **Drug Formulary:** A four-tier formulary applies to prescription drug coverage. Full drug list available as a PDF in the assets folder or through the carrier website.
7. **Spanish documents:** Spanish-language versions of most materials are available and should be referenced for Spanish-speaking members.
8. **HRA (Health Reimbursement Arrangement):** Available for some plans — administered by Igoe. Contact HR for eligibility details.
9. **All document links** use the format: `/assets/[document-name].pdf` relative to the benefits site URL.
